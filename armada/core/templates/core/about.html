Armada is a multi-tenant tool to manage your characters, corporations and alliances. There's a bunch of API tools out there, so we try to focus on that other functionality you'd wish you had, but nobody has made or released to the public. Here's some random screenies of stuff that's already been made.

The usual hurf blurf character page.

A searchable view of your assets.

Stocker lets you keep track of how many items you have in a corporation hangar at a station. You can give other members access to view the specific stock groups you have set up, without giving them access to your corporation API keys or without the ability to view other assets. Nifty for keeping track of logistics.
