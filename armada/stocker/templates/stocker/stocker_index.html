Stocker

Stocker monitors your corporation's inventory stocks. It works by creating a team associated with a corporation API key. After creating the team and selecting your key, you can create watch groups which monitor a list of items at a given location. Lastly, you can add other members of your corporation to your team and assign them different permissions per watch group. Whenever a member views the stock level of a group, your API key will be used to retrieve the assets.
